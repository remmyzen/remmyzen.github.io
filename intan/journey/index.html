First Message

Remmy sends Intan a message for the first time after their mutual friend Nico wants them to get to know each other. First Video Call

Remmy is back in Erlangen, Germany. They are currently 15,954 km and 8 hours apart. Remmy asks Intan for a video call. They have not seen each other since their first message. Intan also jokingly says that she wants to make sure that Remmy is not a robot. They are both nervous, but finally meet on Google Meet for the first time. Remmy uses the robot filter as an inside joke that he is a robot. When they first meet, they play games like Geoguessr, Globle, and Worldle together because they both like geography.

Communication Continues

Even though they are far apart, they communicate intensely and consistently through messages and video calls. They try to understand each other better, align their values, and deepen their feelings for each other. They try to schedule a video call at least once a week. During the week, either Remmy accompanies Intan to study or Intan accompanies Remmy to work, just to keep each other company. On weekends, they watch films, listen to music, or watch badminton together among many other activities. Note that they are not officially a couple, yet...

Love Confession

Intan finishes her assignments for the semester. Remmy finally confesses his love to Intan on a video call, and the feeling is mutual. First Physical Meeting in Brisbane

It is summer break in Germany and winter break in Australia. Yes, they are in different hemispheres, so the seasons are the opposite of each other. Remmy goes on holiday to Indonesia, but also to Brisbane, Australia, to fulfil his promise to Intan. It is in Brisbane that they finally meet physically for the first time. The doubts that once existed about their relationship begin to crumble. Intan is still in the middle of her semester, but is kind enough to accompany Remmy as a tourist. In the same spirit, Remmy accompanies Intan as she studies and goes to her lecture. In spite of that, they still manage to go on picnics, culinary tours, and road trips to Toowoomba, Sunshine Coast, and the Ekka Festival. They had a blast but it is another goodbye for now. Nevertheless, they plan to meet each other again in December.

Trip to Malaysia

Trip to Jakarta

They both fly from Kuala Lumpur to Jakarta. They attend Intan's best friend's wedding and once again go on culinary tours around Jakarta from the list they (mostly Intan) saved together on Instagram.
