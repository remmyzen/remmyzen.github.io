Our Story

All started with a mutual best friend named Nico. Knowing that both of our energy levels were too low to initiate romance on our own, Nico proactively became our middleman instead. Nico’s goal, however, was clearly just to match us up, because that guy did not actually care that we never really lived in the same country. In the beginning, it was Germany (him) and Australia (her), and now it’s Australia (him) and Indonesia (her). Yes, until the day you receive this invitation, we have been running this relationship 90% online. Hahaha (bittersweet laugh).

We bonded quickly over endless virtual Geoguessr and trivia quizzes, where we both showed our competitiveness and pettiness — and, of course, our shared love for badminton matches. Whenever we managed to meet in person, we made sure to make every second count and memorable by taking trips and exploring new places together at a lazy pace — another joy we have in common. So, all in all, thank you still, Nico, and thank you to all of you who have wished us the best along the way.

tl;dr we met through a friend and thought we’d give marriage a go.
