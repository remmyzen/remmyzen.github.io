Remmy Zen

Postdoctoral Researcher at the Theory Division of Max Planck Institute for the Science of Light

       

My full name is Remmy Augusta Menzata Zen. I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute for the Science of Light working under the Munich Quantum Valley initiative on discovering better quantum computers with machine learning.

My main research interest is in applying machine learning to quantum physics, in particular, and science, in general.

I received my PhD degree from the School of Computing, National University of Singapore. My dissertation topic was on transfer learning for neural-network quantum states.
